About the Symposium

The Indo-French symposium "Cross Fertilization between Academia and Industry: Towards Safer and Efficient Catalytic Processes" brings together experts from both countries to foster collaboration and innovation in the field of catalysis.

  • 26 regular lectures from Indian and French delegates
  • 6 short lectures on cutting-edge research
  • Poster session for networking and idea exchange

Hosted at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, this symposium aims to bridge the gap between academic research and industrial applications in catalytic processes. Join us for three days of knowledge sharing, collaboration, and exploration of new frontiers in catalysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Key information about the Indo-French symposium on catalytic processes

What is the full name and focus of the symposium?

The full name is "Cross Fertilization between Academia and Industry: Towards Safer and Efficient Catalytic Processes". It focuses on fostering collaboration between academic research and industrial applications in the field of catalysis.

When and where will the symposium be held?

The symposium will be held from 24th to 26th October 2024 at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus in India.

Who are the keynote speakers?

The symposium features distinguished speakers from both India and France, including experts from institutions like BITS Pilani, IIT Hyderabad, CSIR-NCL, ENSI Caen, and UCCS Lille, among others. Please refer to the delegates section for a full list of speakers and their topics.

What types of sessions will be included in the symposium?

The symposium will include 26 regular lectures (30 minutes each), 6 short lectures (15 minutes each), a poster session, round table discussions, and networking opportunities during meals.

Will there be any industry representation at the symposium?

Yes, the symposium includes speakers from industry, such as representatives from Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) and Reliance Industries Limited, ensuring a balance between academic and industrial perspectives.

Are there any additional activities planned during the symposium?

Yes, the program includes visits to various eminent local institutions in Hyderabad and interactions with faculty members. There will also be informal discussions and networking opportunities during meals.
